Project Overview

Bangla Summarizer is designed to solve the problem of information overload in Bengali digital media. Manually reading through lengthy news articles or documents can be time-consuming. This tool automates the process by extracting the most significant sentences and generating a coherent summary.


Utilizing Python and standard NLP libraries, the system performs tokenization, stop-word removal, and word frequency analysis to rank sentences by importance. It is an essential tool for researchers, students, and anyone looking to digest Bangla content quickly.

The Algorithm

  • Preprocessing: Cleaning text, removing special characters, and tokenizing sentences.
  • Stop Word Removal: Filtering out common words (e.g., এবং, বা, কিন্তু) that carry little meaning.
  • Word Scoring: Calculating Term Frequency (TF) to find significant keywords.
  • Sentence Ranking: Scoring sentences based on the density of significant words.
  • Extraction: Selecting the top N ranked sentences to form the final summary.
